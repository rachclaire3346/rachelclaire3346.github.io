About Me

Hello, my name is Rachel! I am 23 years old, born and raised in Lexington, Kentucky. I am currently studying web development at Awesome Inc. I have a 3 year old Shih Tzu named Howie and a 1 year old cat named Charlie. For fun I love to travel the world, spend time outdoors (my cat and dog love to go hiking with me), and go to music fetivals. I started traveling the world at 13, visiting countries like Fiji, New Zealand, and attending music festivals all across western Europe.

Out of high school I began working on the web team at Lexmark International. When I was first hired, Lexmark was in the process of converting to a new CMS. I was able to help bring partnernet.com and Lexmark.com onto AEM(Adobe Experience Manager). The next project I worked on was merging content from Kofax.com and Readsoft.com after Lexmark acquired portions of the companies. I was able to learn concepts such as SEO, canonical tagging, and site catalysts while working through an Agile Scrum process. I eventually became part of the web support team for both Lexmark.com and Kofax.com where I was able to become familiar with a more HTML heavy CMS, SiteCore.

After working at Lexmark I began working for a small, family owned chiropractic company, Lexington Family Chiropractic. Here I was able to use more of my design skills by creating posters, giveaway signs, and banner images for their website. I was also asked to help solve daily problems in the office. The office manager would voice her concerns or daily difficulties and I would create systems and forms to help the office run more smoothly.
